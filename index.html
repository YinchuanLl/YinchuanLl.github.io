ABOUT ME

I am an AI Researcher at Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab as of September 2020. Before that I received my PhD degree from Beijing Institute of Technology with early graduation honor in 2020. From November 2017 to November 2019, I was a joint training PhD student at Columbia University, where I was supposed to earn my second PhD degree in 2022, but unfortunately it was interrupted due to the COVID-19 pandemic and Proclamation 10043. This is my lifelong regret. Before getting my PhD, I already was a senior technical consultant at Santé Ventures, USA, from December 2019 to August 2020, leading a team to develop a reinforcement learning system for financial stock investment from scratch. My current research interests include machine learning, deep learning and reinforcement learning.
